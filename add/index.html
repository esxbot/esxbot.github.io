Add Bastion to Discord

Add Bastion to Discord, and give awesome perks to your Discord server.

You have two different options here, you can either add the public Bastion bot to your Discord server.
Or you can choose to host Bastion yourself, privately, on your own machine/server and be your own boss.

Add Public Bastion

Music is disabled in the public Bastion due to hosting &amp; maintenace cost for so many Discord servers.

Host Private Bastion

You get to experience every features of Bastion; play high quality music, configure it the way you want, custom avatar, username are just the tip of the iceberg. Whole set of bot owner only commands at the press of your keyboard!
